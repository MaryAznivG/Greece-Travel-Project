Why Greece?

Greece has more than 18 UNESCO Heritage-listed sites

The capital of Greece is named after the goddess Athena, the goddess of wisdom and warfare. According to Greek Mythology, Athena and Poseidon fought over the right to name the city by offering gifts to the citizens, and the Greeks chose Athena to be the Patron of Athens. Because Athena planted a seed in the ground, that grew up to be a lovely olive tree. And the citizens liked this gift better due to it provided them food, oil, and firewood.
